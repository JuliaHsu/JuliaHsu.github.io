Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Information Technology with a concentration in Information Sciences and Technology at George Mason University. My research interests include Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence for social good, computational community data analytics, civic technology and information inequality.

Currently I am a Graduate Research Assistant at Community Informatics Lab at George Mason University.
